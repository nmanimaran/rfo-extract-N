PGI 242.804-71 Reporting requirements

(a) See DoDI 5000. 85, Major Capability Acquisition, for reporting requirements for defense technology projects and acquisition programs. (b)(i) Within four working days after receipt of the contractor's report, the CAO must provide the report and any required comments to the contracting officer and, unless otherwise specified in the contract, the inventory control manager. (ii) If the contractor's report indicates that the contract is on schedule and the CAO agrees, the CAO does not need to add further comments. In all other cases, the CAO must add comments and recommend a course of action.
