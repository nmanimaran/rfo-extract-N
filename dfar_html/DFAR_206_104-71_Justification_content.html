206.104-71 Justification content

(a)(1) In lieu of the threshold at FAR 6.104-2(a) Table 6-1, each justification shall include the information at FAR 6.104-1(a), except for sole-source 8(a) contracts over $100 million (see paragraph (d) of this section). (2) Include the information required by PGI 206.104-71(b) in justifications citing the authority at FAR 6.103-1. (b) In lieu of the threshold at FAR 6.104-1(b), each justification for a sole-source 8(a) contract over $100 million shall include the information at FAR 6.104-1(b).
