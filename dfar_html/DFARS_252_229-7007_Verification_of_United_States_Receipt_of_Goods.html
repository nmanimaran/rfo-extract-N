252.229-7007 Verification of United States Receipt of Goods

As prescribed in 229.402-70(g), use the following clause: VERIFICATION OF UNITED STATES RECEIPT OF GOODS (JUN 1997) The Contractor shall insert the following statement on all Material Inspection and Receiving Reports (DD Form 250 series) for Contracting Officer approval: “I certify that the items listed on this invoice have been received by the United States.” (End of clause)
