209.104-70 Solicitation provision.

Unless the solicitation includes the provision at FAR 52.204-7, insert the provision at 252.209-7002, Disclosure of Ownership or Control by a Foreign Government, in all solicitations, including solicitations using FAR part 12 procedures for the acquisition of commercial products and commercial services, and solicitations using the procedures in FAR part 13, when access to proscribed information is necessary for contract performance.
