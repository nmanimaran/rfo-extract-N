PGI 218.7001-3 Defense or recovery from certain events

For acquisitions that, as determined by the head of the contracting activity, are to facilitate defense against or recovery from  cyber, nuclear, biological, chemical, or 
radiological attack; to facilitate provision of international disaster assistance; or to support response to an emergency or major disaster,  the following requirements do not 
apply : 
  (a)  Policy for unique item identification .  Contractors are not required to provide DoD 
unique item identification if the items are to be used to facilitate defense against or recovery from nuclear, biological, chemical, or radiological attack .  However, contractors 
are not exempt from this requirement if the items are to be used to facilitate defense against or recovery from cyber attack.  See part 211. 
  (b)  Only one offer requirements .  See subpart 215.3.  
  (c)  Approval of determination and findings for time- and-materials or labor -hour 
contracts .  See subpart 216.6.
