243.370-5 Exceptions

unpriced change orders for the purchase of initial spares.  
  (b)  The limitations in  243.370- 4(a) do not apply to unpriced change orders for 
ship construction and ship repair.

and 243.370- 4 for unpriced change orders if the head of the agency determines that
