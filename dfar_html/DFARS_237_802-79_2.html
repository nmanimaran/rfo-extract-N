237.802-79 Private sector notification requirements in support of in-sourcing actions.

(a) Contracting officers will provide written notification to affected incumbent contractors of Government in-sourcing determinations (10 U.S.C. 2463).

(b) See PGI 237.802-79 for notification procedures and for further information in memorandum, “Private Sector Notification Requirements in Support of In-sourcing Actions,” dated January 29, 2013.
