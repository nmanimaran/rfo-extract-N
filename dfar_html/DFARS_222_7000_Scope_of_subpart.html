222.7000 Scope of subpart

(a) This subpart implements Section 8071 of the Fiscal Year 2000 Defense Appropriations Act, Public Law 106-79, and similar sections in subsequent Defense Appropriations Acts. (b) This subpart applies only— (1) To construction and service contracts to be performed in whole or in part within a noncontiguous State; and (2) When the unemployment rate in the noncontiguous State exceeds the national average rate of unemployment as determined by the Secretary of Labor.
