216.505-70 Additional solicitation provisions and contract clause.

(a) Insert the provisions at 252.215-7007, Notice of Intent to Resolicit, and 252.215- 7992, Only One Offer (DEVIATION 2026-O0048), as prescribed at 215.202-71(f) and 215.110-70(c), respectively. Attachment 1 Changes are indicated by a change bar in the right-hand margin.

(b) Insert the clause at 252.216-7010, Postaward Debriefings for Task Orders and Delivery Orders, in competitive negotiated solicitations and contracts, including solicitations and contracts using FAR part 12 procedures for the acquisition of commercial products and commercial services, when a multiple-award contract is contemplated and task orders or delivery orders placed under the contract may be valued at $15 million or more.
