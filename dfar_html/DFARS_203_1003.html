203.1003 Requirements.

(b) Notification of possible contractor violation. Upon notification of a possible contractor violation of the type described in FAR 3.1003(b), coordinate the matter with the following office: Department of Defense Office of Inspector General, Administrative Investigations Contractor Disclosure Program, 4800 Mark Center Drive, Suite 14L25, Arlington, VA 22350-1500. Toll-Free Telephone: 866-429-8011. Website: https://www.dodig.mil/Programs/Contractor-Disclosure-Program/.

(c) Fraud hotline poster. For contracts performed outside the United States, when security concerns can be appropriately demonstrated, the contracting officer may provide the contractor the option to publicize the program to contractor personnel in a manner other than public display of the poster required by 203.1004(b)(2)(ii), such as private employee written instructions and briefings.
