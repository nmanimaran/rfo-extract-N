PGI 210.070 Market research report guide for improving the tradecraft in services

acquisition.  
The “Market Research Report Guide for Improving the Tradecraft in Services 
Acquisition” is designed to aid in  the collection and sharing of standardized market 
research information across the Department.   The guide is available for download at  
https://www.acq.osd.mil/asda/dpc/cp/policy/docs/sa/2017_Market_Research_Guide_(Fi
nal).pdf .
