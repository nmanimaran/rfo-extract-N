PGI 236.202-371 Statutory Fee Limitation

This fee limitation applies only to Departments of the Army, Navy, and Air Force requirements (e.g., if the Department of the Army awards a contract for the Defense 
Logistics Agency or the Department of the Interior, this fee limitation does not apply).  See FAR 15.404- 9(c)(4)(i i) for the fee limitation that applies to other DoD components and civilian 
agencies.
