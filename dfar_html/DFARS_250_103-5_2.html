250.103-5 Processing cases.

(1) At the time the request is filed, the activity must prepare the record described at PGI 250.101-370 (1)(i) and forward it to the appropriate official within 30 days after the close of the month in which the record is prepared.

(3) Contract adjustment boards will render decisions as expeditiously as practicable. The Chair must sign a memorandum of decision disposing of the case. The decision must be dated and must contain the information required by FAR 50.103-6. The memorandum of decision must not contain any information classified “Confidential” or higher. The board's decision will be sent to the appropriate official for implementation.
