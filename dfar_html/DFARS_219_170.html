219.170 Advancing small business growth.

Insert the provision at 252.219-7000, Advancing Small Business Growth, in solicitations when the estimated annual value of the contract is expected to exceed—

(a) The small business size standard, if expressed in dollars, for the North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code assigned by the contracting officer; or

(b) $70 million, if the small business size standard is expressed as number of employees for the NAICS code assigned by the contracting officer.
