216.305-70 Contract clauses.

As required by section 827 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2013 (Pub. L. 112-239), insert the clause at 252.216-7009, Allowability of Legal Costs Incurred in Connection With a Whistleblower Proceeding, in—

(a) Contracts and task orders issued on or after September 30, 2013; and

(b) Task orders entered pursuant to contracts awarded before September 30, 2013, that—

(1) Include the clause at FAR 52.216-7, Allowable Cost and Payment; and

(2) Are modified to include the clause at 252.203-7002, Requirement to Inform Employees of Whistleblower Rights, dated September 2013 or later.
