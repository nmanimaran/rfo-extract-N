211.274-2 Requirements for item unique identification

(a)  DoD item unique identification, or a DoD recognized unique identification 
equivalent, is required for all delivered items, including items of contractor -acquired 
property delivered on line items (see PGI 245.402 -71 for guidance when delivery of 
contractor acquired property is required) .   
  (b)  The primary inventory control activity (PICA)  determines that an item requires 
unique item -level traceabilit y. The PICA will  designate the item as a “DoD serially 
managed item.”  DoD serially managed items are iden tified by the Federal Logistics 
Information System DoD serially managed item indicator set to ‘Y’.   
  (c)  Procedures for identifying items that require unique item -level traceability can 
be found in department of Defense Instruction ( DoDI ) 8320.04, DoD Serially Managed 
Items of Tangible Personal Property and Item Unique Identification 2.0 Standard.
