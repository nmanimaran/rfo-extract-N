(b) Domestic nonavailability determinations.

(3) Defense agencies other than the Defense Logistics Agency.

(B) The Principal Director, Defense Pricing, Contracting, and Acquisition Policy, will forward the request to the Under Secretary of Defense (Acquisition and Sustainment) (USD(A&S)) as appropriate.
