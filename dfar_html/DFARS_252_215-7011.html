252.215-7011 Requirements for Submission of Proposals to the.

Administrative Contracting Officer and Contract Auditor. As prescribed in 215.110-70(e)(2), use the following provision: (JAN 2018) When the proposal is submitted, the Offeror shall also submit one copy each to—

(a) The Administrative Contracting Officer; and

(b) The Contract Auditor. (End of provision)
