PGI 232.7003 Procedures

(a)  DoD officials receiving payment requests in electronic form must  process the 
payment requests in electronic form.  The WAWF system provides the method to electronically process payment requests and receiving reports.  
   (1)  Documents necessary for payment, such as receiving reports, invoice 
approvals, contracts, contract modifications, and required certifications, must be processed 
in electronic form.  
 
  (2)  Scanned documents and other commonly used file formats are only acceptable 
for processing supporting documentation.
