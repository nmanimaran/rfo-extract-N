217.7103-2 Period of agreement

(a)  Master agreements remain in effect until cancelled by either the contractor or 
the contracting officer.  
  (b)  Either the contractor or contracting officer may cancel a master agreement  by 
giving 30 days written notice to the other.  
  (c)  Cancellation of a master agreement does not affect the rights and liabilities 
under any job order existing at the time of cancellation.  The contractor must continue to perform all work covered by any job order issued before the effective date of cancellation of the master agreement.
