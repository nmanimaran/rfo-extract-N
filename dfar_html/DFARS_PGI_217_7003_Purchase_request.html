PGI 217.7003 Purchase request

Ensure that the requiring activity provides all of the following in support of the purchase request— (a) A certification that the property is eligible for exchange and complies with all conditions and limitations of DoD Manual 4140.01, Volume 9, DoD Supply Chain Materiel Management Procedures: Materiel Programs. (b) A written determination of economic advantage indicating— (1) The anticipated economic advantage to the Government from use of the exchange authority; (2) That exchange allowances shall be applied toward, or in partial payment of, the items to be acquired; and (3) That, if required, the exchange property has been rendered safe or innocuous or has been demilitarized; (c) All applicable approvals for the exchange; and (d) A description of the property available for exchange (e.g., nomenclature, location, serial number, estimated travel value).
