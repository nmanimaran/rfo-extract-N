PGI 203.502-2 Subcontractor kickbacks

The DoD Inspector General has designated Special Agents of the following investigative organizations as representatives for conducting inspections and audits under 41 U.S.C. chapter 87, Kickbacks: (a) U.S. Army Criminal Investigation Command. (b) Naval Criminal Investigative Service. (c) Air Force Office of Special Investigations. (d) Defense Criminal Investigative Service.
