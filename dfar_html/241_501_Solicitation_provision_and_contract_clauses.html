241.501 Solicitation provision and contract clauses

(d)(1)  Insert  a clause substantially the same as the clause at FAR 52.241- 7, Change 
in Rates or Terms and Conditions of Service for Regulated Services, when the utility services to be provided are subject to an independent regulatory body.  
   (2)  Insert  a clause substantially the same as the clause at FAR 52.241- 8, 
Change in Rates or Terms and Conditions of Service for Unregulated Services, when the utility services to be provided are not subject to a regulatory body or are subject to a nonindependent re gulatory body.
