PGI 215.404-974 Fee requirements for cost-plus-award-fee contracts.

In developing a fee objective for cost-plus-award-fee contracts, the contracting officer must—

(a) Follow the guidance in FAR 16.402-4 and 216.402-4,

(b) Not use the weighted guidelines method or alternate structured approach,

(c) Apply the offset policy in 215.404-973(b)(2) for facilities capital cost of money, i.e., reduce the base fee by the amount of facilities capital cost of money, and

(d) Not complete a DD Form 1547.
