226.7200 Scope of subpart

This subpart implements section 853 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2004 (Pub. L. 108- 136, 10 U.S.C. 3901 note prec. ).  This subpart does not 
supersede the requirement to use the mandatory sources in FAR part 8 or the small business programs in FAR part 19.
