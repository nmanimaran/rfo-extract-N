237.803-2 Funding and term of service contracts

(a)(i) Personal service contracts for expert or consultant services shall not exceed 1 year. The nature of the duties must be— (A) Temporary (not more than 1 year); or (B) Intermittent (not cumulatively more than 130 days in 1 year). (ii) The contracting officer may enter into a contract, exercise an option, or place an order under a contract for severable services for a period that begins in one fiscal year and ends in the next fiscal year if the period of the contract awarded, option exercised, or order placed does not exceed 1 year (10 U.S.C. 3133).
