206.102-1 Establishing or maintaining alternative sources

(a)  Agencies may use this authority to totally or partially exclude a particular 
source from a contract action.  
  (b)  The determination and findings (D&F) and the documentation supporting the 
D&F must  identify the excluded source .  Include the information at  PGI 206.102-1(b), 
as applicable, and any other information that may be pertinent, in the supporting 
documentation.
