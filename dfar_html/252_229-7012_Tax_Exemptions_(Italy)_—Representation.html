252.229-7012 Tax Exemptions (Italy) —Representation

As prescribed in  229.402- 70
(c)(2), use the following provision:  
  TAX EXEMPTIONS (ITALY) —REPRESENTATION (MAR 2012)  
        (a)  Exemptions .  The United States Government is exempt from payment of — 
                (1)   Imposta Valore Aggiunto (IVA) tax in accordance with Article 72 of the IVA 
implementing decree on all supplies and services sold to United States Military Commands in Italy; and  
                (2)   The other taxes specified in paragraph (c) of the clause DFARS 252.229-
7003, Tax Exemptions (Italy).  
        (b)  Representation .  By submission of its offer, the offeror represents that the offered 
price, including the prices of subcontracts to be awarded under the contract, does not 
 29 
 include the taxes identified herein, or any other taxes from which the United States 
Government is exempt.  
  
(End of provision)
