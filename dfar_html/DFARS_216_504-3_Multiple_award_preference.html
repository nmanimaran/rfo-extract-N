216.504-3 Multiple award preference

(a)(4)(i) The senior procurement executive has the authority to make the determination authorized in FAR 16.504-3(a)(4)(i). (A) In accordance with 10 U.S.C. 3403(d)(3), when making the determination at FAR 16.504-3(a)(4)(i)(A), the senior procurement executive must determine that the task or delivery orders expected under the contract are so integrally related that only a single source can “efficiently perform the work,” instead of “reasonably perform the work” as required by the FAR. (a)(4)(ii) The congressional notification requirement at FAR 16.504- 3(a)(4)(ii) does not apply to DoD. (a)(4)(iii) In accordance with 10 U.S.C. 3403(d)(3), the determination at FAR 16.504-3(a)(4)(iii) is not required if a justification has been executed, in accordance with FAR 6.104 and section 206.104.
