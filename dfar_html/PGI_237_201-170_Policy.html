PGI 237.201-170 Policy

(a)(1) Generally, the determination and finding (D&F) should authorize one contract at a time; however, an authorizing official may issue a blanket D&F for classes of contracts. (2) Prepare each D&F in accordance with FAR 1.5 and include a determination that— (i) The duties are of a temporary or intermittent nature; (ii) Acquisition of the services is advantageous to the national defense; (iii) DoD personnel with necessary skills are not available; (iv) Excepted appointment cannot be obtained; (v) A nonpersonal services contract is not practicable; (vi) Statutory authority, 5 U.S.C. 3109 and other legislation, apply; and (vii) Any other determination required by statutes has been made.
