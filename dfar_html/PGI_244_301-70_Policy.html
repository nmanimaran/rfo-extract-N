    (B)  Correction of material weaknesses .

     (2)  The contracting officer will determine if the contractor has corrected the 
weaknesses and deficiencies.

deficiencies, the contracting officer's notification will be sent to the PSA; auditor; payment 
office; appropriate action officers responsible for reporting past performance at the requiring activities; and each contracting and contract administration office having substantial business with the contractor as appl icable.
