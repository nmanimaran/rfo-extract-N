219.7106 Performance reviews

DCMA will conduct annual performance reviews of all mentor- protege agreements as 
indicated in Appendix I, Section I -113.  The determinations made in these reviews 
contribute to the determinations of amounts of reimbursement, if any, that the mentor firm is eligible to receive in the remaining years of the Program participation term under the agreement .
