206.103-4 International agreement

(c)  Limitations.  Pursuant to 10 U.S.C. 3204(e)(4)(E), the justifications and 
approvals described in FAR 6.104 are not required if the head of the contracting activity 
prepares a document that describes the terms of an agreement or treaty or the written directions, such as a Letter of Offer and Acceptance, that have the effect of requiring the use of other than competitive procedures for the acquisition.
