252.215-7007 Notice of Intent to Resolicit.

As prescribed at 215.202-71(f), use the following provision: NOTICE OF INTENT TO RESOLICIT (JUN 2012) This solicitation provides offerors fewer than 30 days to submit proposals. In the event that only one offer is received in response to this solicitation, the Contracting Officer may cancel the solicitation and resolicit for an additional period of at least 30 days in accordance with 215.202-71(b). (End of provision)
