PGI 229.101-70 Resolving tax problems

(a)  For the military departments, the Defense Logistics Agency, and the Defense 
Contract Management Agency, the members of the DFARS Tax Committee are the designated legal counsel for tax matters within their respective departments/agencies.  
  (b)  Information on fuel excise taxes, including applicability, exemptions, and refunds, is 
available as follows:  
   (i)  The D LA Energy website at https://www.dla.mil/Energy/ provides information on 
Federal,  State, and local excise taxes.   
   (ii)  Internal Revenue Service Publications  510 and 378, available on the Internal 
Revenue Service website at www.irs.gov/publications , provides  information on Federal 
excise taxes.
