240.270-5 Waivers

The Secretary of Defense may waive the prohibition in 2 40.270- 3 on a case -by-case 
basis for a single, one -year period, if the Secretary — 
  (a)  Determines such waiver to be in the national security interests of the United 
States; and  
  (b)  Certifies to the Congressional defense committees that — 
   (1)  There are sufficient mitigations in place to guarantee the ability of the 
Secretary to carry out the covered missions; and  
   (2)  The Secretary is removing the use of covered defense 
telecommunications equipment or services in carrying out such missions.
