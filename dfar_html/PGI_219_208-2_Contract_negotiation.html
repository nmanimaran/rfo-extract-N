PGI 219.208-2 Contract negotiation

  (1)  The agency may negotiate directly with the 8(a) contractor.  The contracting officer is 
responsible for initiating negotiations;  
 (2)  The 8(a) contractor is responsible for negotiating within the time established by the 
contracting officer;  
  (3)  If the 8(a) contractor does not negotiate within the established time and the agency 
cannot allow additional time, the contracting officer may, after notifying the SBA, proceed with the acquisition from other sources;  
  (4)  If requested by the 8(a) contractor, the SBA may participate in negotiations; and  
  (5)  SBA approval of the contract is not required.
