244.201-1 Consent requirements.

(a) In accordance with section 824 of Pub. L. 115-232, notwithstanding the requirements in FAR 44.201-1(a), the contracting officer will not withhold consent to subcontract without the written approval of the program manager, or comparable requiring activity official exercising program management responsibilities, if the contractor has an approved purchasing system.

(i) See PGI 244.201-1(i) for considerations when contracting for information technology, whether as a service or as a supply, that is a covered system or covered item of supply as defined at 239.7301.
