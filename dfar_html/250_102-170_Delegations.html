250.102-170 Delegations

(a)  Military  departments.   The Departments of the Army, Navy, and Air Force 
will specify delegations and levels of authority for actions under the Act and the 
Executive Order in departmental supplements or agency acquisition guidance.  
 
 (b)  Defense  agencies.   Subject to the restrictions on delegations of authority in  
250.102- 1 (b) and FAR 50.102- 1, the directors of the defense agencies may exercise 
and redelega te the authority contained in the Act and the Executive Order. The 
agency supplements or agency acquisition guidance must  specify the delegations 
and levels of authority.
