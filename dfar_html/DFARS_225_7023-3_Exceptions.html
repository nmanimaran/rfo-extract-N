225.7023-3 Exceptions

The restriction in section 225.7023-2 does not apply to acquisitions— (a) Of covered items for use outside of the United States; (b) At or below $200,000; or (c)(1) If the head of the contracting activity determines that a covered item of satisfactory quality and quantity, in the required form, cannot be procured as and when needed from nations other than a covered country to meet requirements at a reasonable price. (2) Include a copy of any such determination in the contract file.
