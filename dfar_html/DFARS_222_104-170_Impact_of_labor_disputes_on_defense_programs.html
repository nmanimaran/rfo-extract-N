222.104-170 Impact of labor disputes on defense programs

(a) Each department and agency must determine the degree of impact of potential or actual labor disputes on its own programs and requirements. For guidance on determining the degree of impact, see PGI 222.104-170. (b) Each contracting activity must obtain and develop data reflecting the impact of a labor dispute on its requirements and programs. Upon determining that the impact of the labor dispute is significant, the head of the contracting activity must submit a report of findings and recommendations to the labor advisor in accordance with departmental procedures.
