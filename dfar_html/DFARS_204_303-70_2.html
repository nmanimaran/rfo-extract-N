204.303-70 DoD reporting thresholds.

(a) Requirement. This section prescribes the requirement to report certain contracted services in accordance with 10 U.S.C. 4505.

(b) Reporting requirements thresholds. Service contractor reporting of information is required in the System for Award Management (SAM) when a contract or order—

(1) Has a total estimated value, including options, that exceeds $3 million; and

(2) Is for services in the following service acquisition portfolio groups (see

(i) Logistics management services.

(ii) Equipment-related services.

(iii) Knowledge-based services.

(iv) Electronics and communications services.
