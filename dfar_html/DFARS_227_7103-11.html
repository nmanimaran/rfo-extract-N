227.7103-11 Contractor procedures and records.

(a) Contractors, and their subcontractors or suppliers that will deliver technical data or computer software with other than unlimited rights, are required to establish and follow written procedures to assure that restrictive markings are used only when authorized and to maintain records to justify the validity of asserted restrictions on delivered data or software.

(b) Contractors are required to maintain sufficient records to justify the validity of restrictive markings on technical data or computer software the contractor must deliver under a Government contract.
