PGI 246.201-71 Aircraft

(a)  The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) has certain responsibilities and 
prerogatives in connection with some commercial aircraft and of aircraft equipment and accessories (Pub. L. 85- 726 (72 Stat 776, 49 U.S.C. 1423)).  This includes the issuance 
of various certificates applicable to design, manufacture, and airworthiness.
