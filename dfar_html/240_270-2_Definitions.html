240.270-2 Definitions

As used in this  section — 
 “Covered defense telecommunications equipment or services ” means — 
  (1) Telecommunications equipment produced by Huawei Technologies Company 
or ZTE Corporation, or any subsidiary or affiliate of such entities;  
  (2) Telecommunications services provided by such entities or using such 
equipment; or  
  (3) Telecommunications equipment or services produced or provided by an entity 
that the Secretary of Defense reasonably believes to be an entity owned or 
controlled by, or otherwise connected to, the government of a covered foreign country.  
 “Covered foreign country ” means — 
  (1) The People's Republic of China; or  
  (2) The Russian Federation.  
  “Covered missions ” means — 
  (1) The nuclear deterrence mission of DoD, including with respect to nuclear 
command, control, and communications, integrated tactical warning and attack 
assessment, and continuity of Government; or  
  (2) The homeland defense mission of DoD, including with respect to ballistic 
missile defense.
