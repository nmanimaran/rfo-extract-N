219.208-2 Contract negotiation.

For sole-source requirements processed under the PA, follow the procedures at PGI 219.208-2.

(a)(1) In lieu of the threshold at FAR 19.208-2(a), the SBA may not accept for negotiation a DoD sole-source 8(a) contract that exceeds $150 million unless the DoD has completed a justification in accordance with the requirements of FAR 6.104 and 206.104-70.
