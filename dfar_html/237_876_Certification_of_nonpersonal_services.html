237.876 Certification of nonpersonal services

(a)  The agency head must  employ procedures to ensure that requirements for 
service contracts are vetted and approved as a safeguard to prevent contracts from being awarded or administered in a manner that constitutes an unauthorized personal services contract.    
  (b)  F ollow the procedures at PGI 237.876, to include substantially similar 
certifications in conjunction with service contract requirements and place the certification in the contract file.   The program manager or other official responsible for 
the requirement, at a level specified by the agency, should execute the certification.
