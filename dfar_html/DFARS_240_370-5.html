240.370-5 Solicitation provision and contract clauses.

(a) Insert the provision at 252.204-7008, Compliance with Safeguarding Covered Defense Information Controls, in solicitations, including solicitations using FAR part 12 procedures for the acquisition of commercial products and commercial services, except for solicitations solely for the acquisition of commercially available off-the-shelf (COTS) items.

(b) Insert the clause at 252.204-7009, Limitations on the Use or Disclosure of Third-Party Contractor Reported Cyber Incident Information, in solicitations and contracts, including solicitations and contracts using FAR part 12 procedures for the acquisition of commercial products and commercial services, for services that Changes are indicated by a change bar in the right-hand margin include support for the Government's activities related to safeguarding covered defense information and cyber incident reporting.

(c) Insert the clause at 252.204-7012, Safeguarding Covered Defense Information and Cyber Incident Reporting, in solicitations and contracts, including solicitations and contracts using FAR part 12 procedures for the acquisition of commercial products and commercial services, except for solicitations and contracts solely for the acquisition of COTS items.

(d) Insert the clause at 252.240-7997, NIST SP 800-171 DoD Assessment Requirements, in solicitations and contracts, task orders, or delivery orders, including those using FAR part 12 procedures for the acquisition of commercial products and commercial services, except for those that are solely for the acquisition of COTS items.
