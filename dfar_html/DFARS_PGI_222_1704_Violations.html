PGI 222.1704 Violations

(a)(1) If the contracting officer receives information indicating that the contractor, a contractor employee, a subcontractor, or a subcontractor employee has failed to comply with the requirements of the clause at FAR 52.222-50, then— (i) Through the contracting officer's local commander or other designated representative, immediately notify the Combatant Commander responsible for the geographical area in which the incident has occurred; and (ii) Provide information about any investigation and enforcement to— (A) Program Manager, DoD CTIP Policy and Programs Support at https://ctip.defense.gov/ or by email to dodctip@mail.mil; and (B) Defense Pricing, Contracting, and Acquisition Policy (Contract Policy) by email to osd.pentagon.ousd-a-s.mbx.asda-dp-c-contractpolicy@mail.mil. (2) Reports may be made to the DoD Inspector General Hotline at http://www.dodig.mil/hotline/ or 800-424-9098. (3) Reports also may be made to the National Human Trafficking Hotline at 1-888- 373-7888 or to the Hotline website at https://humantraffickinghotline.org/.
