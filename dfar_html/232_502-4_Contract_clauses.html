232.502-4 Contract clauses

232.502- 4-70  Additional clauses.  
  (a)  Insert  the clause at 252.232 -7002, Progress Payments for Foreign Military Sales 
Acquisitions, in solicitations and contracts that — 
   (i)  Contain FMS requirements; and  
   (ii)  Provide for progress payments.  
  (b)  Insert  the clause at 252.232 -7004, DoD Progress Payment Rates, instead of 
Alternate I of the clause at FAR 52.232- 16, if the contractor is a small business concern.  
  (c)  Insert  the clause at 252.232 -7018, Progress Payments —Multiple Lots, to 
authorize separate progress payment requests for multiple lots.
