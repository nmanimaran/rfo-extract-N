252.225-7028 Exclusionary Policies and Practices of Foreign Governments

As prescribed in 225.7307(b), use the following clause: (APR 2003) The Contractor and its subcontractors shall not take into account the exclusionary policies or practices of any foreign government in employing or assigning personnel, if— (a) The personnel will perform functions required by this contract, either in the United States or abroad; and (b) The exclusionary policies or practices of the foreign government are based on race, religion, national origin, or sex. (End of clause)
