237.802-72 Contracts for management services

DoD may award a services contract for acquisition support functions that support  the 
development or production of a major system (Section 802 of Pub. L. 110- 181), only if — 
  (a)  The contract prohibits the contractor from performing inherently governmental 
functions;  
  (b)  The DoD organization responsible for the development or production of the 
major system ensures that Federal employees are responsible for determining — 
   (1)  Courses of action to be taken in the best interest of the Government; and  
   (2)  Best technical performance for the warfighter; and  
  (c)  The contract prohibits  the prime contractor from advis ing or recommending  the 
award of a contract or subcontract for the development or production of the major system to an entity it owns in whole or in part.
