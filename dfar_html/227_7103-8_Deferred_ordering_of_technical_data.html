227.7103-8 Deferred ordering of technical data

Order as necessary  any technical data, computer software or computer software 
documentation generated in the performance of the contract or subcontract at  any time 
until three years after acceptance of all items (other than technical data or computer software) under the contract or contract termination, whichever is later.  See the clause 
at 252.227 -7995 , Deferred Ordering of Technical Data or Computer Software.
