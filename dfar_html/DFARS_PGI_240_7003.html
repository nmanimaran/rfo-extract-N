PGI 240.7003 Prohibitions relating to Chinese military companies.

(a) The Department of State is the lead agency responsible for the regulations governing the export of defense articles, which are identified on the United States Munitions List. The Department of State has issued the International Traffic in Arms Regulations, which implement the Arms Export Control Act (22 U.S.C. 2751) and include the United States Munitions List (USML).

(b) The official version of the International Traffic in Arms Regulations can be found in Title 22, Parts 120 through 130, of the Code of Federal Regulations (22 CFR 120-130), published by the U.S. Government Publishing Office. The current version is available at https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-22/chapter-I/subchapter-M. The Changes are indicated by a change bar in the right-hand margin Department of State also publishes an on-line version at https://www.pmddtc.state.gov/ddtc_public/ddtc_public.

(c) For the disposition for items, or goods, or to include items or goods incidental to services associated with recycling services requirements, see the latest update to the DoD Manual 4160.21, Volume 2, Defense Materiel Disposition: Property Disposal and Reclamation located at https://www.dla.mil/Disposition-Services/DDSR/Quick- Links/DEMIL-Codes/.
