216.601-470 Solicitation provisions.

Insert the provision at 252.216-7002, Alternate A, with FAR 52.216-29, Time-and- Materials/Labor-Hour Proposal Requirements—Other Than Commercial Acquisition with Adequate Price Competition, in solicitations contemplating the use of a time-and-materials or labor-hour contract type for other than commercial products or commercial services if the price is expected to be based on adequate competition.
