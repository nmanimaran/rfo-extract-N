240.7003-0 Scope.

(a) This section implements the following:

(1) Section 1211 of the National Defense Authorization Act (NDAA) for Fiscal Year (FY) 2006 (Pub. L. 109-163).

(2) Section 1243 of the NDAA for FY 2012 (Pub. L. 112-81).

(3) Section 1296 of the NDAA for FY 2017 (Pub. L. 114-328). Changes are indicated by a change bar in the right-hand margin

(4) Section 857(b) of the NDAA for FY 2023 (Pub. L. 117-263).

(5) Section 805 of the NDAA for FY 2024 (Pub. L. 118-31).

(6) Section 851 of the NDAA for FY 2025 (Pub. L. 119-159).

(b) See PGI 240.7003 for additional information relating to this section, including terms used in this section.
