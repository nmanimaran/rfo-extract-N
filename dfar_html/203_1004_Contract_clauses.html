203.1004 Contract clauses

(a)  Insert  the clause at 252.203 -7003, Agency Office of the Inspector General, in 
solicitations and contracts that include the FAR clause 52.203- 13, Contractor Code of 
Business Ethics and Conduct.  
  (b)(2)(ii)  Unless the contract is for the acquisition of a commercial product or 
commercial service, insert  the clause at 252.203- 7004, Display of Hotline Posters, in lieu 
of the clause at FAR 52.203- 14, Display of Hotline Poster(s), in solicitations and 
contracts, if the contract value exceeds $7.5 million . If the Department of Homeland 
Security (DHS) provides disaster relief funds for the contract, DHS will provide information on how to obtain and display the DHS fraud hotline poster (see FAR 3.1003).
