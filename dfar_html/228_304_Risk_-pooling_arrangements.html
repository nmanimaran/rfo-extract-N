228.304 Risk -pooling arrangements

DoD has established the National Defense Projects Rating Plan, also known as the Special Casualty Insurance Rating Plan, as a risk -pooling arrangement to minimize the 
cost to the Government of purchasing the liability insurance listed in FAR 28.307 -2.  
Use the plan in accordance with the procedures at PGI 228.304 w
hen it provides the 
necessary coverage and is more advantageous than commercially available coverage.
