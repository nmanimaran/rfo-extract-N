PGI 206.104-71 Justification content

Justifications citing the authority at FAR 6.103- 1 to permit the use of other than full and 
open competition, shall -- 
   (1)  Include the results of the request for information or sources sought notice posted 
in accordance with PGI 206.103- 170 (unless the requirement to post has been waived); and  
   (2)  For non -competitive follow -on acquisitions of supplies or services previously 
awarded on a non -competitive basis, include a copy of the previous justification to assist the 
approval authority in determining whether the planned actions to remove any barr iers to 
competition cited on the previous justification were completed.
