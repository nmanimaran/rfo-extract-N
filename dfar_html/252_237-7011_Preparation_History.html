252.237-7011 Preparation History

As prescribed in 237.7003(a) and (a)(8), insert  the following clause:  
 
PREPARATION HISTORY ( DEC 1991)  
 For each body prepared, or for each casket handled in a group interment, the Contractor shall state briefly the results of the embalming process on a certificate 
furnished by the Contracting Officer.  
 
(End of clause)
