PGI 247.370 DD Form 1384, Transportation Control and Movement

(a) The transportation office of the shipping activity prepares the DD Form 1384 to accompany all shipments made through a military air or water port, in accordance with DoD 4500.9-R, Defense Transportation Regulation, Part II, Chapter 203. (b) DTR 4500.9-R, Defense Transportation Regulation, Part II, Chapter 203 is available at https://www.ustranscom.mil/dtr/part-ii/dtr_part_ii_203.pdf.
