231.205-6 Compensation for personal services

(f)(1) Bonuses and extra payments paid by the contractor to an employee related to restructuring after a business combination are not allowed costs on DoD contracts if paid with funds from fiscal year 1996 appropriations or later ( Section 8122 of Pub. L. 104-61, and similar sections in subsequent Defense appropriations acts). This limitation does not apply to severance payments or early retirement incentive payments. (“Business combination” and “restructuring costs” are defined at 231.205- 70(b).) (m)(1) Fringe benefit costs that violate laws, employer-employee agreements, or contractor established policy are unallowable.
