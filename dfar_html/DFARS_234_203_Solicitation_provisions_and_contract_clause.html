234.203 Solicitation provisions and contract clause

Except for contracts subject to the exemption at 234.201 paragraph (10), for cost or incentive contracts valued at $50 million or more, and for other contracts for which EVMS will be applied in accordance with 234.201(1)(iii) and (iv)— (1) Insert the provision at 252.234-7998, Notice of Earned Value Management System, in the solicitation; and (2) Insert the clause at 252.234-7999, Earned Value Management System, instead of the clause at FAR 52.234-4, Earned Value Management System, in the solicitation and contract.
