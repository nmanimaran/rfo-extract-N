211.272 Use of proprietary specifications or standards.

To comply with section 888(b)(1) of FY 2017 NDAA, a justification and approval is required to use proprietary specifications and standards—

(a) When using sealed bidding or negotiated acquisition procedures (see 206.103-1); or

(b) When using the simplified procedures for certain commercial products or commercial services at FAR 12.201-1 (see part 212).
