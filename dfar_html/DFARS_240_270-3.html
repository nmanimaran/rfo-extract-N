240.270-3 Prohibition.

In addition to the prohibition at FAR 40.202(d), unless the covered defense telecommunications equipment or services are subject to a waiver described in 240.270-5 the contracting officer must not procure or obtain, or extend or renew a contract (e.g., exercise an option) to procure or obtain, any equipment, system, or service to carry out covered missions that uses covered defense telecommunications equipment or services as a substantial or essential component of any system, or as critical technology as part of any system.
