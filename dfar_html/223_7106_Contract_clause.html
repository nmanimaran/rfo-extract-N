223.7106 Contract clause

Insert  the basic or the alternate of the clause at 252.223 -7006, Prohibition on Storage, 
Treatment, and Disposal of Toxic or Hazardous Materials, in all solicitations and contracts which require, may require, or permit contractor access to a DoD installation.  
  (a) Insert  the basic clause, unless a determination is made under 223.7104(a)(10).  
  (b) Insert the alternate I clause when the Secretary of the military department 
issues a determination under the exception at 223.7104(a)(10).
