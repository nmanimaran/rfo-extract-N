PGI 232.7101 Policy and procedures

Background.  The Internal Revenue Service (IRS) is authorized to collect overdue taxes through a continuous levy up to 100 percent of the debt on certain vendor contract payments disbursed by the Defense Finance and Accounting Service.  The levy is continuous until the overdue taxes are paid in full, or other arrangements are made to 
satisfy the debt.  Contractors are required to promptly notify the procuring contracting officer when a tax levy that may result in an inability to perform the contract is imposed, so that the contracting officer and the Principal Director, Defense Pricing, Contracting, 
and Acquisition Policy  (DPCAP), can take appropriate action to mitigate any possible 
adverse effect on national security.

contractor that the tax levy may result in an inability to perform  the contract , the
