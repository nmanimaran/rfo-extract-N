252.227-7995 Deferred ordering of technical data or computer software

(DEVIATION 2026-O0036) As prescribed at 227.7103-6(h), use the following clause: (DEVIATION 2026-O0036) (FEB 2026) In addition to technical data or computer software the Contractor must deliver elsewhere in this contract, the Government may, at any time during the performance of this contract or within a period of three years after acceptance of all items (other than technical data or computer software) the Contractor must deliver under this contract or the termination of this contract, order any technical data or computer software generated in the performance of this contract or any of its subcontracts. When the technical data or computer software is ordered, the Contractor shall be compensated for converting the data or computer software into the prescribed form, for reproduction and delivery. The Contractor’s obligation to deliver technical data related to an item from a subcontractor expires three years after the Contractor accepts the final delivery of that item from that subcontractor. The Government’s rights to use the data or computer software shall be pursuant to the clause at 252.227-7989, Rights in Technical Data, Computer Software, and Computer Software Documentation—Other Than Commercial Products or Commercial Services, of this contract. (End of clause)
