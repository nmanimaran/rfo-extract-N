216.403-270 Performance incentives.

(a) See PGI 216.403-270 for guidance on establishing performance incentives.

(b) Ensure requirements for the payment of incentive fees or the imposition of penalties are included in the solicitation for the engineering and manufacturing development or production of a weapon system, including embedded software, if the program manager or comparable requiring activity official exercising program manager responsibilities includes—

(1) Provisions for the payment of incentive fees based on achievement of design specification requirements for reliability and maintainability of weapons systems under the contract; or

(2) The imposition of penalties to be paid by the contractor to the Government for failure to achieve such design specification requirements (10 U.S.C. 4328).
