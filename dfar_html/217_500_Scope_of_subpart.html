217.500 Scope of subpart

(a)  Unless more specific statutory authority exists, the procedures in FAR subpart 
17.5 and this subpart apply to all purchases, except micro -purchases, made for DoD by 
another agency.  This includes orders under a task or delivery order contract entered into by the other agency.  (Pub. L. 105 -261, Section 814.)  
  (b)  A contracting activity from one DoD Component may provide acquisition 
assistance to deployed DoD units or personnel from another DoD Component.  See PGI 217.502- 1 for guidance and procedures.
