235.174-1 Indemnification under research and development contracts

(a)  Under 10 U.S.C. 3861, and with authorization from t he Secretary concerned, 
contracts for research and/or development may include provisions  for indemnification of 
the contractor or subcontractors for—  
   (1)  Claims by third persons (including employees) for death, bodily injury, or 
loss of or damage to property;  
   (2)  Loss of or damage to the contractor's property ; and   
    
  (3)  Liability, loss or damage that — 
 35 
    (i)  Results from a risk that the contract defines as “unusually hazardous” ; 
 
   (ii)  Arises from the direct performance of the contract; and 
    (iii)  Is not compensated by insurance or other means.  
  (b)  Clearly define the specific unusually hazardous risks to be indemnified.  Submit 
this definition for approval with the request for authorization to grant indemnification.  Include the approved definition in the contract.
