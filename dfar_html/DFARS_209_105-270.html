209.105-270 Inclusion of determination of contractor fault in the Integrity Records module in CPARS.

In accordance with section 834 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2011 (Pub. L. 111-383), if the contractor or a subcontractor at any tier is not subject to the jurisdiction of the U.S. courts, and the DoD appointing official that requested a DoD investigation makes a final determination that the contractor’s or subcontractor’s gross negligence or reckless disregard for the safety of Government personnel caused serious bodily injury or death of such personnel, then enter information regarding the determination in the Integrity Records module in CPARS within 3 days of receiving it. Information posted in the Integrity Records module in CPARS will be publicly available.
