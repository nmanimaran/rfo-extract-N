211.274-3 Requirements for valuation

(a)  The statement of work or requirement document must require contractors to  
identify the Government's unit acquisition cost for all deliverable end items to 
which item unique identification applies.  
 
 (b)  The Government's unit acquisition cost is â€” 
   (1)  For fixed -price type line, subline, or exhibit line items, the unit price 
identified in the contract at the time of delivery;  
   (2) For cost -type or undefinitized line, subline, or exhibit line items, the 
contractor's estimated fully burdened unit cost to the Government at the time of delivery; and  
   (3)   For items delivered under a time- and-materials contract, the 
contractor's estimated fully burdened unit cost to the Government at the time of delivery.  
  (c)  The Government's unit acquisition cost of subassemblies, components, and 
parts embedded in delivered items must  not be separately identified.
