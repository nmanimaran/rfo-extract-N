PGI 217.670 Procedures

(a)(3)(i)  Departments and agencies must  ensure full awareness of the total costs of all 
fees associated with use of a non -DoD contracting vehicle.  To ensure full awareness, the 
DoD acquisition official must  require the servicing agency to─  
    (A)  Provide notification if its acquisition approach will use another agency’s 
contract vehicle; and  
    (B)  Identify all fees (direct and indirect) associated with the assisted acquisition.  
   (ii)  The DoD acquisition official must document in the business decision the 
rationale to continue with the assisted acquisition given the total fees (direct and indirect) involved.
