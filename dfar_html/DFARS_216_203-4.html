216.203-4 Contract clauses.

(a) In addition to the conditions at FAR 16.203-4(a), insert the clause at FAR 52.216-2, Economic Price Adjustment—Standard Supplies, when—

(1) The total contract price exceeds the simplified acquisition threshold; and

(2) Delivery or performance will not be completed within 6 months after contract award.

(b) In addition to the conditions at FAR 16.203-4(b), insert the clause at FAR 52.216-3, Economic Price Adjustment—Semistandard Supplies, when—

(1) The total contract price exceeds the simplified acquisition threshold; and

(2) Delivery or performance will not be completed within 6 months after contract award.

(c) In addition to the conditions at FAR 16.203-4(c), insert the clause at FAR 52.216- 4, Economic Price Adjustment—Labor and Material, when—

(1) The total contract price exceeds the simplified acquisition threshold; and

(2) Delivery or performance will not be completed within 6 months after contract award. Attachment 1 Changes are indicated by a change bar in the right-hand margin.

(d)(2) Follow the procedures at PGI 216.203-4 when inserting an economic price adjustment clause based on cost indexes of labor or material.
