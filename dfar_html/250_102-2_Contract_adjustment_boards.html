250.102-2 Contract adjustment boards

The Departments of the Army, Navy, and Air Force each have a contract 
adjustment board .  The board consists of a Chair and not less than two nor more 
than six other members, one of whom may be designated the Vice- Chair .  A 
majority constitutes a quorum for any purpose and the concurring vote of a majority 
of the total board membership constitutes an action of the board. Alternates may be 
appointed to act in the absence of any member.
