252.204-7006 Billing Instructions—Cost Vouchers.

As prescribed in 204.202-79(b), insert the following clause: Billing Instructions—Cost Vouchers (MAY 2023) When submitting a request for payment using a cost voucher, the Contractor shall—

(a) Identify the contract line item(s) on the payment request that reasonably reflect contract work performance; and

(b) Separately identify a payment amount for each contract line item included in the payment request. (End of clause)
