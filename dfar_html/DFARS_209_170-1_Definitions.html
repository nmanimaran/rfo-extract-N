209.170-1 Definitions

As used in this section— “Confucius Institute” means— (1) Any program that receives funding or support from— (i) The Chinese International Education Foundation; or (ii) The Center for Language Exchange Cooperation of the Ministry of Education of the People’s Republic of China; or (2) Any cultural institute directly or indirectly funded by the government of the People’s Republic of China. “Institution of higher education” has the meaning given in 20 U.S.C. 1002.
