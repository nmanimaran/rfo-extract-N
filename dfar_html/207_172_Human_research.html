207.172 Human research

Any DoD component sponsoring research involving human subjects — 
  (a)  Is responsible for oversight of compliance with 32 CFR Part 219, Protection of 
Human Subjects; and  
  (b)  Must have a Human Research Protection Official, as defined in the clause at 
252.235- 7004
, Protection of Human Subjects, and identified in the DoD component’s 
Human Research Protection Management Plan.  This official is responsible for the oversight and execution of the requirements of the clause at 
252.235- 7004  and must  be 
identified in the written acquisition plan.
