227.7202-3 Rights in commercial computer software or commercial computer software documentation

(a) The Government has the rights specified in the license under which the Government obtained the commercial computer software or commercial computer software documentation. (b) No contract clause governs the Government’s rights in commercial computer software or commercial computer software documentation. (c) If the Government has a need for rights not conveyed under the license customarily provided to the public, negotiate with the contractor to determine acceptable terms for transferring such rights.
