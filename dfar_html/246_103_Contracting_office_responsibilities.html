246.103 Contracting office responsibilities

(1)  The contracting office must coordinate with the quality assurance activity 
before changing any quality requirement.  
 
 (2)  The activity responsible for technical requirements may prepare instructions 
covering the type and extent of Government inspections for acquisitions that are complex, have critical applications, or have unusual requirements .  Follow the 
procedures at PGI 246.103
(2) for preparation of instructions.
