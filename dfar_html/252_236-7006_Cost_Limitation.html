252.236-7006 Cost Limitation

As prescribed in 236. 101-71(b)(4), use the following provision:  
 
COST LIMITATION (JAN 1997)  
  (a)  Certain items in this solicitation are subject to statutory cost limitations.  The 
limitations are stated in the Schedule.  
  (b)  An offer which does not state separate prices for the items identified in the 
Schedule as subject to a cost limitation may be considered nonresponsive.  
  (c)  Prices stated in offers for items subject to cost limitations shall include an 
appropriate apportionment of all costs, direct and indirect, overhead, and profit.  
  (d)  Offers may be rejected which â€” 
   (1)  Are materially unbalanced for the purpose of bringing items within cost 
limitations; or  
   (2)  Exceed the cost limitations, unless the limitations have been waived by the 
Government prior to award.  
 
(End of provision)
