212.203 Evaluation.

(a)(1)(i) Include an evaluation factor regarding supply chain risk (see subpart 239.70) when acquiring information technology, whether as a service or as a supply, that is a covered system, is a part of a covered system, or is in support of a covered system, as defined in 239.7001.

(ii) When acquiring transportation or transportation related services, see PGI 212.203.
