PGI 240.7003-4 Procedures.

(a) Best practices and information.

(1) Additional information and resources are available at the following Government agency websites:

(i) Federal Bureau of Investigation, Counterintelligence. The China Threat, available at https://www.fbi.gov/investigate/counterintelligence/the-china-threat.

(ii) Office of the Director of National Intelligence, National Counterintelligence and Security Center – Threat Assessments to Disrupt and Defeat the Adversary, Economic Espionage, available at https://www.odni.gov/index.php/ncsc- what-we-do/ncsc-threat-assessments-mission/ncsc-economic-espionage.

(iii) Office of the Director of National Intelligence, National Counterintelligence and Security Center – Supply Chain Threats, Supply Chain Risk Changes are indicated by a change bar in the right-hand margin Management, available at https://www.odni.gov/index.php/ncsc-what-we-do/ncsc- supply-chain-threats.

(iv) Office of the Director of National Intelligence, National Counterintelligence and Security Center – Research Security, available at https://www.dni.gov/index.php/safeguarding-science/research-security and Cybersecurity & Infrastructure Security Agency, National Coordinator for Critical Infrastructure Security and Resilience, available at https://www.cisa.gov/.

(2) Consult with the Industrial Base Policy, Global Investment and Economic Security (GIES), Policy and Engagement Office as required for the relevant policy guidance and technical support for the requiring activity officials performing program management responsibilities, to assist affected businesses, institutions, and organizations subject to the prohibitions: https://www.businessdefense.gov/gies/index.html.

(b) Entity list. For further guidance and information on the prohibited Chinese military company list of entities, any non-specially designated nationals (SDN) Chinese military-industrial complex (CMIC) company, or any other covered company covered by the USML, or the 600 series of the Commerce Control List (CCL), see the following:

(1) The current section 1260H list is published in the Federal Register and at https://www.war.gov/News/Publications/.

(2) The NS-CMIC List includes entities currently identified in the non- specially designated nationals (SDN) Chinese military industrial complex companies list (NS-CMIC List), published in the Federal Register by the Department of the Treasury or on the website of the Department of the Treasury’s Office of Foreign Assets Control

(OFAC) is accessible through the following page on OFAC's website: https://ofac.treasury.gov/.

(i) Contracting officers or requiring activity officials can access the latest changes to the NS-CMIC list that includes entities currently identified in the Non-SDN, NS-CMIC list, or for information on the Chinese military company list of prohibited entities at the Department of Treasury OFAC search tool website: https://ofac.treasury.gov/sanctions-list-service.

(ii) The latest changes to the NS-CMIC list are provided prior to their publication in the Federal Register; requiring activity officials can rely on the changes indicated in the consolidated screening list (CSL): https://www.trade.gov/consolidated- screening-list.
