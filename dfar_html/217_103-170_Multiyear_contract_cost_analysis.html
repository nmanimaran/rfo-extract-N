217.103-170 Multiyear contract cost analysis

Before awarding a multiyear contract, the head of the agency must compare the cost of that contract to the cost of an annual procurement approach, using a present value analysis.  Only award the multiyear contract if the analysis shows that it will result in a lower cost (10 U.S.C. 3501(l)(7); section 8008(a) of Pub. L. 105- 56, and similar sections 
in subsequent DoD appropriations acts).
