PGI 229.7002-1 Value added tax.

(a) U.S. Government purchases qualifying for tax relief are equipment, materials, facilities, and services for the common defense effort and for foreign aid programs.

(b) To facilitate the resolution of issues concerning specific waivers of import duty or tax exemption for U.S. Government purchases (see PGI 229.7002-3), contracting offices must provide the name and activity address of personnel who have been granted warranted contracting authority to His Majesty’s (HM) Revenue and Customs (HMRC) at the following address: HM Revenue and Customs Parliament Street 100, London SW1A 2BQ].
